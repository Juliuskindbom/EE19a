JoJo Parts

This is the story the first 5 JoJo parts

JoJo Part 1 is a story about Jonathan Joestar and his rival Dio Brando. Dio decides to become a vampire in search of strength, power, wealth and women. He is a bad person and everything Jonathan despises.

JoJo part 2 is about Jonathan's son Joseph Joestar and his hamon training so he can defeat 3 ancient warriorgods that are about to enslave the world.

JoJo part 3 is aout Joseph Joestars grandson Jotaro Kujo and his gang including Joseph, Kakyoin, Avdol and Polnareff.They travel around the world to save Jotaro's mother by killing Dio that has taken over Jonathans body.

in part 4 we follow Josuke Higashikata which is Josef's son. Joseph cheated on his wife once and she got pregnant with him. Murders start happening in the a town called Morioh, Josuke and his friends decides to find the killer.

In part 5 we get to follow Giorno Giovanna who is the son of Dio Brando. Giorno sees how some scummy gangs sells drugs to kids and he does not like that. So he decides to join a gang and become the gangster with the highest rank a "Gangstar"
